Community Markets

FN&TT hosts warm, inclusive community markets featuring local artists, youth vendors, handmade goods, and live performances. These events uplift small businesses and create a welcoming space for creativity and connection.

Includes:

  • Local artisans and makers
  • Youth vendor booths
  • Live music and dance
  • Food, wine, beer, and bourbon cocktails

Immersive Themed Events

FN&TT creates atmospheric, ritual-inspired events that blend décor, music, movement, and storytelling. These experiences are designed to transport attendees into a world of artistry and intention.

Past and planned events include:

  • Echoes Hafla
  • Seasonal ritual-themed gatherings
  • Collaborative performances with local musicians

Fire-Free Events

Unless explicitly stated and approved, all FN&TT events are fire-free. Fire performance is reserved for vetted venues, trained dancers, and controlled environments that meet our safety standards.
