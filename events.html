Meet Your Instructor — Drake von Trapp

Drake von Trapp is a belly dance historian, researcher, teacher, and performer — recognized for his pioneering research on male belly dancers in the U.S. He holds a BA and MA in Dance and a post-master's in Multicultural Women's and Gender Studies from Texas Woman's University, and is pursuing a PhD in Performance Studies at UT Austin.
