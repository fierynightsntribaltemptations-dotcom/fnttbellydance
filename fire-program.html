About FN&TT

A fusion belly dance and ritual movement collective rooted in creativity, community, and transformation. Based in Northern Kentucky.

Meet Firestorm

Firestorm (Chris Rhodes) is a creative ritualist, advanced fusion belly dance choreographer, curriculum architect, and community event leader based in Dry Ridge & Verona, Kentucky.

With a foundation in symbolic movement, fire arts, and immersive performance design, Firestorm blends technical precision with ritual storytelling to create unforgettable experiences for students and audiences alike.

Their work spans choreography, curriculum systems, branding, event management, and community market leadership — all rooted in a deep commitment to safety, artistry, and community.

Our Story

Fiery Nights & Tribal Temptations Belly Dance LLC was created as a home for dancers who crave depth, expression, and a sense of belonging. FN&TT blends fusion belly dance technique, theatrical expression, ritual movement, and fire artistry into a cohesive, intentional practice.

Our approach is grounded in community, personal empowerment, and the belief that dance is both a craft and a transformative ritual. Whether you're stepping into your first class or preparing for advanced performance pathways, FN&TT offers a space where you can grow, explore, and ignite your creative fire.

Our Values

Community: We cultivate a supportive, inclusive environment where dancers uplift one another.

Creativity: Fusion dance thrives on innovation, experimentation, and personal expression.

Safety: Especially within our fire program, safety and responsibility are foundational pillars.

Ritual: Movement is a grounding practice — a way to connect with self, intention, and artistry.

What We Offer

FN&TT provides classes, workshops, community events, and a structured fire program designed to support dancers at every stage of their journey in Dry Ridge, Verona, and all of Northern Kentucky.
