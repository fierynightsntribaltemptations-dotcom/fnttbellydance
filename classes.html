Foundations

New to belly dance? This is your starting point. FN&TT Foundations covers posture, basic isolations, traveling steps, musicality, and the movement vocabulary that everything else at Fiery Nights & Tribal Temptations builds from.

This class is relaxed, encouraging, and designed for real beginners. No experience needed!

Perfect for: Complete beginners, returning dancers, anyone wanting to strengthen their fundamentals.

Fusion Technique

Ready to go deeper? Fusion Technique explores layered movement, creative footwork, combinations, and the expressive styling that makes fusion belly dance so unique. Drills, short combos, and creative exploration — challenging enough to push you forward, fun enough to keep you coming back.

Perfect for: Dancers with some experience ready to expand their skills.

Intentional Movement

Slower. Deeper. More expressive. This class blends soft fusion movement, breathwork, and mindful dance into something that feels less like a workout and more like a reset — for your body and your mind. Think of it like yoga, but with belly dance.

Perfect for: All levels — especially anyone looking for a calmer, more expressive dance experience.

Fire Pathway Prep

Curious about fire dance? Fire Pathway Prep is a no-flame class that teaches prop handling, safety awareness, and the choreography skills you need before ever touching a lit prop. Required before moving into supervised fire training.
