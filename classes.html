Foundations

New to belly dance? This is your starting point. FN&TT Foundations covers posture, basic isolations, traveling steps, musicality, and the movement vocabulary that everything else at Fiery Nights & Tribal Temptations builds from.

This class is relaxed, encouraging, and designed for real beginners. No experience needed — just a willingness to try something new!

Perfect for: Complete beginners, returning dancers, and anyone wanting to strengthen their fundamentals.

FN&TT Level: Embers 🔥

Fusion Technique

Ready to go deeper? Fusion Technique at Fiery Nights & Tribal Temptations explores layered movement, creative footwork, combinations, and the expressive styling that makes fusion belly dance so unique.

This class includes drills, short combos, and creative exploration — challenging enough to push you forward, fun enough to keep you coming back.

Perfect for: Dancers with some belly dance experience who are ready to expand their skills.

FN&TT Level: Flames 🔥🔥

Intentional Movement

Slower. Deeper. More expressive. This class blends soft fusion movement, breathwork, and mindful dance into something that feels less like a workout and more like a reset — for your body and your mind.

Think of it like yoga, but with belly dance. Perfect for anyone who wants to move with more presence and intention, no matter their experience level.

Perfect for: All levels — especially anyone looking for a calmer, more meditative dance experience.

FN&TT Level: All levels welcome 🔥

Fire Pathway Prep

Curious about fire dance? This is where it starts at Fiery Nights & Tribal Temptations. Fire Pathway Prep is a no-flame class that teaches you prop handling, safety awareness, and the choreography skills you need before ever touching a lit prop.

This class is required before moving into supervised fire training — and it's where you'll find out if fire dance is the right path for you.

Required before: Supervised light sessions and fire performance.

FN&TT Level: Firestorm / Inferno pathway 🔥🔥🔥🔥
