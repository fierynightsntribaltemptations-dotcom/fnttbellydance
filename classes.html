Beginner Belly Dance — Foundations

New to belly dance? This is your starting point. Foundations covers posture, basic isolations, traveling steps, musicality, and the movement vocabulary that everything else at FN&TT builds from.

This class is relaxed, encouraging, and designed for real beginners. No experience needed — just a willingness to try something new! And yes, wine is welcome. 🍷

Perfect for: Complete beginners, returning dancers, and anyone wanting to strengthen their fundamentals.

FN&TT Level: Embers 🔥

Fusion Technique

Ready to go deeper? Fusion Technique explores layered movement, creative footwork, combinations, and the expressive styling that makes fusion belly dance so unique.

This class includes drills, short combos, and creative exploration — challenging enough to push you forward, fun enough to keep you coming back.

Perfect for: Dancers with some belly dance experience ready to expand their skills.

FN&TT Level: Flames 🔥🔥

Intentional Movement

Slower. Deeper. More expressive. This class blends soft fusion movement, breathwork, and mindful dance — perfect for anyone who wants to move with more presence and intention.

Think of it like yoga, but with belly dance. All levels welcome.

FN&TT Level: All levels 🔥

Fire Pathway Prep

Curious about fire dance? Fire Pathway Prep is a no-flame class that teaches you prop handling, safety awareness, and the choreography skills you need before ever touching a lit prop.

Required before: Supervised light sessions and fire performance.

FN&TT Level: Firestorm / Inferno pathway 🔥🔥🔥🔥
