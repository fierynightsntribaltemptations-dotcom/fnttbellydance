About Fiery Nights & Tribal Temptations

Fiery Nights & Tribal Temptations Belly Dance — FN&TT — is a fusion belly dance studio based in Dry Ridge and Verona, Kentucky. We offer classes, workshops, performances, and community events for dancers of all experience levels.

Our studio is warm, welcoming, and built around community. Whether you've never danced before or you've been dancing for years, FN&TT has a place for you.

Classes at FN&TT

Fiery Nights & Tribal Temptations offers belly dance classes for every level — from total beginners to experienced dancers ready to explore fire pathway training. Classes are held in Dry Ridge and Verona, Kentucky.

Drop-in and class packs available. Contact us for current schedule and pricing.
