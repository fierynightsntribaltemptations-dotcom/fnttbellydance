About Fiery Nights & Tribal Temptations

Fiery Nights & Tribal Temptations Belly Dance — FN&TT — is a fusion belly dance studio based in Verona and Dry Ridge, Kentucky. We offer classes, workshops, performances, and community events for dancers of all experience levels.

Our studio is warm, welcoming, and built around community. Whether you've never danced before or you've been dancing for years, FN&TT has a place for you.
