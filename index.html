Foundations

A grounding introduction to fusion belly dance technique. Learn posture, isolations, traveling steps, musicality, and the FN&TT movement vocabulary.

Perfect for: Beginners, returning dancers, and anyone wanting to strengthen fundamentals.

Level: Embers

Fusion Technique

A deeper dive into layered movement, footwork, combinations, and expressive fusion styling. Includes drills, short combos, and creative exploration.

Perfect for: Dancers with some experience who want to expand their artistry.

Level: Flames

Ritual Movement

A grounding, intention-driven class blending slow fusion, breathwork, and symbolic movement. Explore dance as a meditative and expressive ritual.

Perfect for: All levels seeking depth, embodiment, and emotional expression.

Level: All levels welcome

Fire Pathway Prep

A non-flame class designed to prepare dancers for the FN&TT Fire Program. Includes cold-prop training, safety protocols, prop handling, and choreography integration.

Required before: Supervised light sessions and fire performance approval.

Level: Firestorm / Inferno pathway
